Gate Valves

Gate valves are primarily designed to start or stop the flow of liquids. They are ideal for applications where a straight-line flow of fluid and minimum flow restriction are needed. Titan Flow Valves offers high-quality gate valves with superior sealing and durability.

  • Robust construction for long service life
  • Full-bore design ensuring minimal pressure drop
  • Available in multiple materials and pressure ratings
  • Ideal for oil & gas, chemical, and water industries

Key Features

High Durability

Built with corrosion-resistant materials to withstand harsh industrial conditions.

Precision Sealing

Advanced sealing technology ensures zero leakage under high pressure.

Low Maintenance

Designed for minimal wear and tear, reducing downtime and maintenance costs.

Applications

Gate valves are used across a variety of industries for their ability to handle high pressure and temperature, as well as corrosive environments.

  • Oil and Gas Pipelines
  • Chemical Processing Plants
  • Power Generation Facilities
  • Water Treatment Plants
  • Marine and Offshore Operations
